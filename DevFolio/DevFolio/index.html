About me

Amirul Islam is a Post-Doctoral Researcher at the Visual Artificial Intelligence Laboratory @ Oxford Brookes University. He is the project leader of MAESTRO Jr. He has completed his PhD from School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering (CSEE) of University of Essex, United Kingdom, majoring in Computing and Electronic Systems in 2022. He completed his Master of Science from Kookmin University, South Korea in August 2017 where he worked as a graduate research student from September 2015 to August 2017.
